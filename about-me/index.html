Hey!

Privately, I do more random things. I play video games and board games, I enjoy cooking and tech-heavy things. I also watch a lot of videos and listen to music; Sub Zero Project is nice :). I'm also a furry as you already probably noticed.

Have fun on my corner of the internet, cheers :3
